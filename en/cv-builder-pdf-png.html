Build CV and preview

Why PDF?

PDF keeps the CV layout stable. Employers, agencies and online application forms can open it without changing margins or spacing. This makes it the safest file format for most professional applications.

With CVFast.app you can preview the CV before unlocking download, so you can fix content and layout problems first.

Why PNG?

PNG is not a replacement for PDF, but it is useful. You can save the CV image on a phone, send it quickly in a message or keep a visual copy in your gallery.

Many people manage job applications from a smartphone, so having both formats makes the finished CV easier to use.
