cvfast.app

CV for Germany, Austria and Switzerland

cvfast.app helps job seekers create a clear CV in English or German for applications in Germany, Austria, Switzerland and other European markets.

What makes it useful?

  • Choose English or German CV language.
  • Use a clean structure with profile, work experience, skills, education and contact details.
  • Preview the CV live before downloading the PDF.
  • No account and no server upload of CV data.

This tool does not guarantee employment. It helps you prepare a readable CV document faster.
