Privacy Policy

cvfast.app is a browser-based resume and CV builder. The app does not require an account and does not upload your CV content to our server.

What stays on your device

The information you type into the builder, including name, contact details, work experience, skills and optional photo, is stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage.

PDF generation

The PDF is generated in your browser. Your CV text is not sent to our server for PDF creation.

Payment/support

Payment support is handled by PayPal. We do not store card numbers, bank details or PayPal passwords.

Device/browser limits

If you clear browser data, use another browser or switch devices, your saved CV data and PDF unlock may be lost.
